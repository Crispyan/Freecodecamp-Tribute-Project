Cristiano Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo thanking the fans after his bicycle kick against Juventus.

Here are some facts about C.Ronaldo.

  • His full name is Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro.

  • He was born on 5 February 1985 in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal.

  • He is 1.87 m (6 ft 2 in).

  • Famously, he wears the number 7.

  • He’s won five Ballon d’Or awards (player of the year).

  • His father, José Dinis Aveiro, died of an alcoholism-related liver condition at age 52 in September 2005 when Ronaldo was 20.

  • He became father to a son in June 2010. Ronaldo announced that he had full custody. Ronaldo has never publicly revealed the identity of his son’s mother.

  • In June 2017, Ronaldo confirmed on Facebook that he had become the father to twins. Five months later, his girlfriend Georgina Rodriguez gave birth to their daughter.

  • Ronaldo was diagnosed with a racing heart at age 15. He underwent an operation to treat his condition.

  • He began his senior club career playing for Sporting CP, before signing with Manchester United at age 18 in 2003.

  • He is playing for Juventus right now.

  • He is better than Messi.
